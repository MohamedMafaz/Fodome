Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fodome?
Fodome is a food delivery service that connects you with your favorite restaurants and delivers food right to your doorstep.
How does Fodome work?
To use Fodome, simply download our app or visit our website, create an account, select the restaurant and food items you want to order, and checkout. Our delivery partners will then deliver your food to you as soon as possible.
How much does Fodome cost?
Fodome charges a small fee for delivery, which varies depending on your location and the restaurant you order from.
Can I cancel my order?
If you need to cancel your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible.
